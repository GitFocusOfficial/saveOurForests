About Our Advocacy

Save Our Forests is a student-led advocacy group committed to raising awareness about the dangers of deforestation. Our mission is to educate people on how deforestation affects biodiversity, contributes to climate change, and disrupts ecosystems. We believe that through collective efforts, we can help reduce deforestation and protect our forests for future generations.
